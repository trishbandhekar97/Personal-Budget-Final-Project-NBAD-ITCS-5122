Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new budget in the app?

Creating a new budget is easy! Just click on the 'Add Budget' button on the dashboard, fill in the details like budget name, amount, and category, and hit 'Save'. Your new budget will be ready to use immediately.

 Can I track expenses in different currencies?

Currently, only USD is supported. But in the future, we will introduce different currencies

 Is my financial data secure with this app?

Security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to ensure that all your financial data is stored securely and remains confidential.

 What happens if I go over my budget?

If you exceed your budget, the app will notify you based on the alert settings you've chosen. You can then review your expenses and adjust your budget or spending as needed.
